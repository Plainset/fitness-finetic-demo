Before you enquire

A couple of things worth knowing

What should I include in my enquiry?

Let Jai know your main goal (e.g. general fitness, weight loss, muscle gain, or yoga), your preferred location (studio, home, park or online), and roughly what times of day suit you.

Which package should I start with?

Most new clients start with the Start Up package (3 sessions, from £120) to try things out before committing to a longer Personal Training or Yoga Pass block — see the Training & Pricing page for full details.
