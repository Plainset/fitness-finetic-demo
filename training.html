Personal Training

One-to-one sessions tailored to your goals, body type and current fitness — held at a private home studio, your own home, garden or workplace. Sessions combine bodyweight exercises, equipment and weights, with all necessary equipment provided.

Suitable for complete beginners through to more experienced exercisers, with a customised plan and personalised lifestyle and nutrition guidance built around you.

Local parks · Your garden

Outdoor Personal Training

Fresh air, more space and a change of scenery — sessions run in local parks or your own garden, with the same one-to-one coaching, motivation and safe technique guidance as a studio session.

A good option if the day-after-work motivation dip is real, or you simply prefer to train outside.

Yoga

Yoga is self-awareness resulting in a union of mind, body and soul — realigning bones, easing muscle tension, and building strength, flexibility and balance through posture, breath and movement.

1

Warm-up

Low-impact movement to release the joints and limber up muscle groups before the main sequence.

2

Sequencing

Breath and movement combine to engage the muscles and cardiovascular system, safely building through the session.

3

Cool-down

Variation and cool-down to close the session, progressing to more challenging work as you advance.
